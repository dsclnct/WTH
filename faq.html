1. What is WTH?

What The Hack is a hackathon conducted by The Google Developers Student Club. The applicants will be asked to solve a problem with a time constraint of 48 hours using their skills in the disciplines of web development, Application development, Machine learning, the Internet of things, etc. Moreover, the event shall also include webinars and would be facilitated by speeches of guests whose knowledge and experience shall be of great use. Waiting for you at the event!!!

2. Who can participate in WTH?

The event would see no restriction based on region, age, educational institution, etc. WTH is OPEN TO ALL and anyone who may solve the given problem and present a useful and practical output is welcome to join us at the event.

3. How do I participate?

You may participate through the direct link circulated to you and if you have none don't worry, just head out to wthack.netlify.app and register. For any queries feel free to contact us.

4. How will I know my registration is approved?

You will receive a confirmation e-mail from us immediately after the completion of your registration, so make sure you provide us with all the correct information.

5. Would Covid restrictions affect my participation?

No, not at all. Your safety matters to us. The whole event is online. You can be a part of the hackathon event from the safe confines of your home.

6. How much does it cost to participate?

WTH's Hackathon is absolutely free of cost. The only input you pay is your knowledge and ideas

7. Do I need prior programming experience?

Not really!! This is a team game, and all contestants are the same in competitions. So here, it's better if you are experienced but if not, you learn and grow with us.

8. What are the perks of joining this event as a participant?

For making a successful carrier in Tech... We not only need to develop our technical skills but also our soft skills as well as team collaboration with effectual communication ....and This hackathon also helps in increasing the network, and in the resume as well...

9. What can I create?

Tell you what creation has no limits. There are problem statements available on the website and you can choose from them. You can create a website, an app, or whatever innovative you can think of according to the caliber of your group.

10. Can I bring my previous project to the hackathon?

No!!! projects built from scratch in the competition will be considered, but Yes you can kick off to create something totally new and different using your experience along with your knowledge, wouldn’t that be awesome!!.

11. Can we form teams?

Yes!! WTH is completely a war between alliances. Participants can form armies of 2 to 4 persons and together fight to win.
